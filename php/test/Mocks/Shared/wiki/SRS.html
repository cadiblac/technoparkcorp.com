Actors

{ActorUser}: a human being with a web browser

{ActorAdmin}: is {ActorUser} who is responsible for management of other {ActorUser}-s

{SUD}: is system under development

Interfaces

{IfWeb}: HTTP interface between {ActorUser} and {SUD} mostly used by {R1}, {UC1} and {R3}.

Use Cases

{UC1}: {ActorVisitor} browses {WebSite} and becomes {ActorUser}.

Functional Requirements

{R4} [c1,i5]: {ActorVisitor} can browse static HTML pages of {WebSite}.

{R1} [must]: {ActorUser} can manage {UserAccount} and {UserInvoice}-s.

{R2} [out,i3]: {ActorAdmin} can view and print {FinanceReports}.

{R3} [c3]: {ActorAdmin} can CRUDL {UserAccount}-s.

Quality of Service (QOS) Requirements

{QOS1}: {SUD} shall handle 100 online connections.

{QOS2} [out,i5]: {SUD} shall work with one server and 100Gbit connection.
