Vision

Statement: There is a strong marketing opportunity to automate an experience earned by ATG during the last 5 years of doing manual "betting arbitrage". The sports betting marketplace is still extremely inefficient and there are arbitrage opportunities (called "arbs") between two sports books somewhere in the world every second of every day. It is a risk-free arbitrage because there is an overlap in pricing for the exact same event, in the exact same market, with the exact same rules. We are creating an automated algorithmic trading software, based on years of experience, that will find these short-lasting opportunities across the globe and with lightening-fast execution bet both sides of the opportunity within seconds.

Product: Semi-automated betting comparison robotic software system, that compares betting rates, discovers arbs, and places bets. The system is intended for private use by a limited number of managers, mostly in-house staff of ATG. The system is a pure "web system" to be hosted on a server in Internet.

Actors:

  • Bettor: Betting web site, e.g. pinnaclesports.com, ladbrokes.com, williamhill.com, and bwin.com. In first version of the system we will use two websites and one sport only.
    • F1: "Feed XML" Bettor can feed the system with XML-formatted betting lines, according to its internal structure of data, the system can receive said data and parse them before processing, the system can understand betting lines, store them in the database and prepare for analysis.
  • Manager: Representative of ATG controlling the system results
    • F2: "Confirm Bets" Manager can review arbs discovered, can review bets suggested by the system, can confirm bets, can monitor how the system is placing bets with bettors (in automated mode)
    • F3: "Monitoring" Manager can review system statistic reports, can review performance reports, can change system configuration parameters without changing of source code, can make corrections to the core algorithm/strategy of the system.

Quality:

  • Reliability: Due to the nature of the business and high probability of financial risks the system shall be extremely reliable in terms of financial transactions. No event either from end-user or from technical equipment shall lead to invalid financial transactions executed.
  • Extendability: Since the betting strategy is very volatile and not fixed in time, the system shall be easily configurable and changeable. Adding of new bettors, new strategy mechanisms, new protocols shall be done fast and seamlessly.
