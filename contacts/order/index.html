    Online Order

    Contact us to get a quotation for your software project. The process is simple:

    1. You contact us online
    2. One of our Project Managers gets back to you
    3. We solicit your requirements (Informal Request)
    4. We develop Vision
    5. You confirm that the Vision covers the whole scope
    6. We estimate the project, you get ROM Estimate
    7. We give you Schedule, and Staffing estimates
    8. We together sign a Contract
    9. You make a first payment (Retainer)
    10. We start the Project and finish it in time, according to the SRS
